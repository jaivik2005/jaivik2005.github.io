About Me

Passionate Developer & Lifelong Learner

I'm a Computer Engineering student who believes in the power of technology to create meaningful change. My journey in web development started with curiosity and has evolved into a passion for building innovative solutions that bridge the gap between complex technology and user-friendly experiences.

My Journey

My interest in technology began during my early school years when I first discovered the magic of creating something from nothing - just lines of code. What started as simple HTML pages has evolved into complex full-stack applications and AI-integrated solutions.

Currently pursuing my B.E. in Computer Engineering at Birla Vishwakarma Mahavidyalaya, I'm constantly expanding my knowledge in web technologies, artificial intelligence, and modern development practices.

My Vision

I envision a future where technology seamlessly integrates with human needs, making complex tasks simple and inaccessible features available to everyone. My goal is to contribute to this future by building applications that are not only technically excellent but also intuitively designed.

I believe in continuous learning, staying updated with emerging technologies, and sharing knowledge with the developer community through educational content and open-source contributions.
